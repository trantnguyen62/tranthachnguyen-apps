Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

DevOps Mastery ("the App") is developed by Tran Nguyen. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

Information We Collect

DevOps Mastery is designed with privacy in mind. The App:

  • Does not collect personal information. We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, or any other personal data.
  • Does not track your location. The App does not access or store your location data.
  • Does not use analytics or tracking services. No third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools are integrated into the App.
  • Stores study progress locally. Your flashcard progress, quiz scores, and preferences are stored only on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional premium subscriptions and a lifetime purchase through Apple's In-App Purchase system. All payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information, credit card details, or Apple ID.

Subscription management (including cancellation) is handled through your Apple ID settings.

Data Storage

All study content (flashcards, quiz questions, and commands) is bundled within the App and available offline. Your study progress is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to any server.

Third-Party Services

The App does not share any data with third parties. The only external service used is Apple's StoreKit for processing In-App Purchases, which is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. The App is an educational tool suitable for all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
