How to Play

🎮

Controls

Arrow Keys / WASD - Move your ship

1, 2, 3, 4 - Select answer (or click)

Space - Fire weapon

P - Pause game

👾

Gameplay

Enemy ships approach carrying DevOps questions

Shoot enemies first for bonus points, then answer their question to destroy them

Wrong answers or timeouts deal damage to your ship

Build answer streaks to multiply your score

Defeated enemies may drop ❤️ health or 🛡️ shield power-ups

⭐

Scoring

+100 - Correct answer

+50 - Speed bonus (< 5 seconds)

+50 - Shooting down an enemy

×1.5, ×2, ×2.5… - Streak multiplier (every 3 correct in a row)

−20 HP - Wrong answer

−15 HP - Question timeout
