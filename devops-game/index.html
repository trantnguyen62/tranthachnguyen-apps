How to Play

🎮

Controls

Arrow Keys / WASD - Move your ship

1, 2, 3, 4 - Select answer (or click)

Space - Fire weapon

P - Pause game

👾

Gameplay

Enemy ships approach — each one carries a DevOps question

Shoot an enemy first to weaken it, then answer its question correctly to destroy it

Wrong answers or timeouts deal damage to your ship; correct answers score points

Answer 3 in a row to start a streak — your score multiplier increases every 3 correct

Destroyed enemies may drop ❤️ health or 🛡️ shield power-ups

⭐

Scoring

+100 - Correct answer

+50 - Speed bonus (< 5 seconds)

+50 - Shooting down an enemy

×1.5, ×2, ×2.5… - Streak multiplier (every 3 correct in a row)

−20 HP - Wrong answer

−15 HP - Question timeout
